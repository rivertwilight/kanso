Professor Luo Xiang's "The Details of the Rule of Law" is an accessible legal popularization book that helps ordinary readers understand the spirit of the rule of law through real cases and vivid language.

The author explores the humanity, justice, and fairness behind the law with his unique perspective and humorous writing style. The book contains both explanations of legal principles and reflections on social phenomena, allowing readers to grasp the essence of the rule of law in a relaxed reading experience.

Professor Luo Xiang emphasizes that the law is not just a set of rules, but a tool for protecting everyone's rights. This book helps us establish a mindset of the rule of law and learn to view social issues through a legal lens.

## Insights

This book can be considered my legal enlightenment book, making me truly realize that the law is not just a collection of legal provisions.

## The Craft Behind the Code

Software development shares deep roots with traditional craftsmanship — intentional choices, iterative refinement, and a pursuit of elegance.

## Aesthetics in Architecture

Great software architecture, like great buildings, balances form and function. The invisible structure matters as much as the visible interface.

## Expressiveness and Constraint

Art thrives within constraints. Programming languages, performance budgets, and platform limitations shape creative expression the way a canvas size shapes a painting.

## The Role of Taste

Taste — knowing what to leave out — separates functional software from truly great software. Simplicity is a deliberate artistic choice.

## Software as a Medium

Code is a medium for thought. Like prose or music, it communicates ideas, encodes worldviews, and reflects the values of its creator.

## The Human Experience

The best software is designed around human emotion and experience, not just utility. Delight, surprise, and calm are valid design goals.

## Why It Matters

Treating software as art elevates the standards we hold ourselves to — and ultimately produces better tools for everyone.
